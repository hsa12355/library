Station 6: Read and Ride

In today’s diverse reading styles, reading can be dynamic and fun. Here, noise-free pedal bikes let you exercise while you read;

the uniquely shaped spinning chairs serve as art installations and playful relaxation toys. Colorful lounge chairs provide students a comfortable and cozy resting space.

This area is also an important venue for audiovisual screenings and open discussion events, creatively blending reading with everyday life.
