Entering the library is very convenient; Yuan Ze University students can use their student ID card or the Yuan Ze app pass to access and borrow books.

Stepping through this entrance, you officially enter a historic and multifunctional smart space. The library was established in 1989, moved to Building 5 in 1997, and expanded with a new building in 2006. It covers approximately 10,000 square meters and offers over 1,100 seats for reading.

The library is divided into three floors: The basement (B1) houses Chinese, Japanese, and Western language book collections, a dense storage area, seminar rooms, an indoor garden, and a Japanese dry landscape Zen garden; the first floor hosts the borrowing and reference desks, new book displays, digital learning area, thesis collection, English reading section, and a vibrant reading zone; the second floor contains current Chinese and Western periodicals, popular magazines, picture book and comic sections, and an aesthetic exhibition space.
