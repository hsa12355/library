A tranquil corner featuring a Japanese Karesansui Zen garden made of fine sand and rocks.

“Mountains” are symbolized by stones, while “water” is represented by sand and gravel, with patterns on the sand surface resembling flowing water.

This garden conveys a peaceful and calm atmosphere through its static elements. The design intention is for every visitor to the library to let go of worries, calm their minds, and focus on the sea of books.
