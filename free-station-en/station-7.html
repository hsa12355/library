The second floor’s current periodicals and popular periodicals areas house hundreds of Chinese and Western periodicals, covering a wide range of subjects to satisfy various academic and personal interests.

Every year, the library holds a "Periodical Giveaway" event, allowing students to take home their favorite past periodicals for free, letting knowledge leave the shelves and enter everyday life.

Please note that periodicals on the second floor are for in-library use only and cannot be borrowed.
