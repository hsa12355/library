Entering the library is quick and easy. Yuan Ze students can enter and borrow books by scanning their student ID or using the digital pass on the Yuan Ze mobile app.

Through this entrance, you officially step into a smart learning environment rich in history and functionality. The Yuan Ze University Library was founded in 1989, relocated to the Fifth Building in 1997, and expanded with a new wing in 2006. The library now covers about 10,000 square meters and provides over 1,100 reading seats.

The library spans three floors: B1 houses Chinese, Japanese, and Western collections, compact stacks, seminar rooms, an indoor garden, and a Zen rock garden. The first floor includes the circulation desk, reference services, new arrivals, digital learning zone, thesis area, English readers section, and vibrant reading spaces.
