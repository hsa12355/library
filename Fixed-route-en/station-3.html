This is where the newest books are freshly presented. Once placed on the new arrivals shelf, they are immediately available for borrowing.

The area is equipped with cozy sofas where students often sit and become completely immersed in reading, losing track of time.

When books are due, you can return them at the front desk. Alternatively, there's a 24-hour book drop located to the right of the main entrance, allowing convenient returns even outside library hours.
