Located on the second floor, the Current and Popular Periodicals area houses hundreds of Chinese and Western journals covering a wide range of disciplines and interests.

Each year, the library holds a “Periodical Giveaway” event, allowing students to take home past issues for free—bringing knowledge from the shelves into everyday life.

Please note that all periodicals on the second floor are for in-library use only and are not available for checkout.
