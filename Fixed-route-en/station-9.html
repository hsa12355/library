Station 9: Compact Shelve

This area is the hidden treasure trove behind the library's collection.

To address the issue of limited shelving space and expand the book collection, the library has a compact stacks area that stores lesser-used but highly valuable books.

Unlike standard fixed bookshelves, this area uses movable shelving. By simply turning the wheel on the side, shelves slide open, allowing readers to easily locate their desired books—an experience akin to “turning the shelves to unlock the door to knowledge.”
