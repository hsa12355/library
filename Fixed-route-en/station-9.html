A tranquil corner featuring a Japanese Karesansui Zen garden made of fine sand and rocks.

“Mountains” are symbolized by stones, while “water” is represented by gravel, with the sand’s patterns resembling flowing water.

This garden conveys a serene and calm atmosphere through its still elements. The design aims to help every visitor to the library let go of worries, calm their mind, and focus on the sea of books.
